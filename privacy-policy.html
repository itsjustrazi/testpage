This Privacy Policy describes how Razi Property JB, operated under OD Legacy Realty Sdn Bhd (E(1)2004), collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, submit enquiries, or interact with our advertisements on platforms such as Meta (Facebook/Instagram) and TikTok.

By using our website or submitting your information through our lead forms, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

01

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Phone number / WhatsApp number
  • Email address (where provided)
  • Property preferences (unit type, budget range, purchase timeline)
  • Enquiry details submitted via contact forms or Meta/TikTok lead forms

We may also automatically collect certain technical data when you visit our website, such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, through cookies and analytics tools.

02

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for legitimate property consultation and marketing purposes:

  • To respond to your property enquiries and arrange viewing appointments
  • To provide information about property projects relevant to your interests
  • To contact you via phone, WhatsApp, or email regarding properties you have expressed interest in
  • To send you updates on new property launches, promotions, and relevant market information
  • To improve our website, services, and advertising effectiveness
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
We will not use your information for any purpose other than property-related consultation and marketing without your explicit consent.
03

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Property developers: Where you have expressed interest in a specific project, we may share your details with the developer solely to facilitate your enquiry or booking
  • Team members: Within Razi Property JB / Homura team for the purpose of following up on your enquiry
  • Legal compliance: Where required by Malaysian law or regulatory authority
  • Service providers: Third-party tools (e.g. CRM, WhatsApp automation) used solely to manage and respond to enquiries
04

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and accessible only to authorised personnel.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable Malaysian law.

05

Your Rights

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Withdraw consent for us to process your data at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided in Section 8.

06

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and to measure the performance of our advertisements.

  • Meta Pixel: We use the Meta Pixel to track website visits and measure ad campaign performance on Facebook and Instagram
  • Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

07

Third-Party Services

Our website and advertisements may contain links to third-party websites or services, including Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, and property listing platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

When you submit a lead form through Meta or TikTok advertising platforms, your information is also subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Meta Privacy Policy: facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • TikTok Privacy Policy: tiktok.com/legal/page/row/privacy-policy
08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of our website or submission of your information after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related enquiries, data access requests, or to withdraw your consent, please contact us directly.
